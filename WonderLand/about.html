About Wonderla Theme Park

Wonderla Amusement Park, home to India's best in class themed entertainment offers world-class service to guests from all over the country and the globe.

Meticulously designed themes at Wonderla weave an interesting Indian storyline through its much-loved home-grown characters called Stars of Wonderla.

An escapade through this Adventure Park shall leave memories etched for a lifetime.

Wonderla has curated many first of its kind offerings rearing to deliver an unmatched Amusement Park experience for one and all! Truly a place where the fun never ends.

The fantastic thing about Wonderla theme park is "There's a Whole Lot for Every Lot!". A wide range of Wonderla's themed rides and attractions include Kiddie rides, Family rides, rides for Grown up's. Right from Nitro's adrenaline-pumping experience to getting family and friends to help rescue Mr. India from Mogambo's terror, Wonderla Amusement park engages you in interesting ways.

And for the kids, you can fly high with Tubbby Takes Off or indulge in some fun water splashing aboard Splash Ahoy.

All this and more, put together including Wonderla's uniquely designed attractions, therefore, makes up for a great day of immersive entertainment experience that promises extraordinary fun, joy, thrill, adrenaline, and everything in and around it to our guests! To tickle everyone's taste buds is the wide range of lip-smacking cuisines served across the multiple in-park restaurants.

Guests can opt for a great Staycation at Novotel Wonderla which makes for a wonderful bundled package with the Theme Park!

A comfortable 90 min drive from Mumbai or Pune via the Mumbai-Pune Expressway, Wonderla is located near Lonavala at Khopoli and is a must-visit Adventure Park.

Located amidst picturesque and serene Sahyadri hills away from the hustle and bustle of the city, Wonderla serves a much-needed dose of break time over and again!

Frequenty Asked Questions

>   Is Wonderla Open?

>   How much do Wonderla Theme Park tickets cost?

Check our `Tickets and Offers` page for the latest Wonderla ticket pricing and related offers.


>   Do you need to book Wonderla tickets in advance?

It is recommended to book your tickets well ahead of time either online or by calling us on 022-62552929 to help skip ticketing queue and fast-track your entry to the park.


>   Can we buy tickets at Wonderla?

Yes. You can buy Tickets at the Park Ticket Counter on arrival. However, online bookings will assure savings upfront.


>   How can I get a ticket discount at Wonderla?

our `Tickets and Offers` shows all the available current deals and discounts


>   Do we need to pay extra for amusement park rides?

Your ticket entitles you unlimited access to all Theme Park rides and attractions.


>   Will the amusement park Wonderla offer group discounts?

We have various deals suited to different needs. You can find the details under the `Tickets and Offers` section.


>   What hotels are near Wonderla?

Novotel is the closest hotel to Wonderla as it is part of our campus and offers luxurious stay.


>   What attractions are near Wonderla amusement park?

One can enjoy different points of Lonavala which is only a 20 min drive from Wonderla or explore the varied caves hidden in the Sahyadri range that surrounds Wonderla.


>   Does Wonderla ticket include food?

Your ticket entitles you unlimited access to all Theme Park rides and attractions only. To save big, you can Pre-book the food voucher as an Add-on.


>   What is an express ticket in Wonderla?

Express ticket allows one-time preferred access to select rides and attractions by skipping ride queue at the Theme Park.


>   What is the best time to visit Wonderla?

Wonderla is an All-season destination. You can plan your visit as per your comfort for any day in the year.


>   Is the camera allowed in Wonderla?

Guests can bring their personal cameras for leisure photography. DSLR cameras with detachable lenses are allowed. However, the Park is not responsible for any damage or loss. Action cameras are allowed, however accessories are not. Some attractions have restrictions on usage and carriage of photography equipment.


>   What should we wear to Wonderla an amusement park?

We recommend wear something comfortable preferably western clothing which reduces the chances of your clothes getting stuck in a speedy ride. Clothing with rude, vulgar, or offensive language or graphics is not permitted, and shirts cannot be turned inside-out as a solution.


>   Is water bottle allowed in Wonderla?

Yes.

Bike - INR 50
Car - INR 100
Bus - INR 300

Yes! You can stay at Novotel Wonderla.
